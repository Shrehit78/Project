Training programme on food and agro processing at VIT

9th January 2018

VIT Technology Business Incubator (VIT-TBI) is organising a six-week non-residential training programme on food and agro processing from January 29.The training programme will cover essentials of entrepreneurship, basics of marketing, finance and HR, and provide exposure to various facets of food processing techniques, quality control, certification processes and hospitality industry, and includes industry visits.

VIT Chancellor to lead Indian Economic Association conference

10th January 2018

Founder-Chancellor of the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) Dr G Viswanathan has been unanimously elected as president of the centenary second year conference of the Indian Economic Association.
